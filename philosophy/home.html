 This particular philosophical journal began paddling among the loons. Why loons? Loons are with the water, Nature’s source and birthing place. Loon’s can fly free, also Nature’s source, our universal instinct of understanding and love. Loon’s mate for life, Nature’s understanding of symbiotic relationships. Loon’s live in a community, Nature’s understanding of synchronicity.
