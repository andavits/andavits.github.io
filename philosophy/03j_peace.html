Peace is Without Boundaries

War is when everything is in size extra large. It starts with boundaries. A man made concept.
War Sum Up: Music, Manga, Machines (by Latvia Radio Choir) 

Peace is unassuming. It has no boundaries; no man made concepts. It just is.
PEACE: IMAGINE

I was born with an intuitive understanding that the world will not cave in on me...and then lost it growing up... and spent my middle-aged years "going back" to my initial intuitive understanding.

Boundaries had grown all around me.
Boundaries I created myself, surrounded my inner light. My personal war.
Boundaries others made around me. Injustices - outer wars. 

I learned that boundaries simply dissolve with love.
PEACE: MAKE LOVE TO THE ENEMIES

Instrumenti - Peace from Instrumenti on Vimeo.

To do this, first I had to learn what love was in general. Then I had to learn how forgiveness works. The rules in scriptures are by definition, boundaries, so did not work. Three was another way, and I will talk about it on another blog.
