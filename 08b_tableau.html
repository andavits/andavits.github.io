What type of data viz to use?
Financial Times' Chart Doctor
  • DEVIATION: diverging bar | diverging stacked bar | spline | surplus/deficit filled line
  • CORRELATION: scatterplot | column & line timeline | connected scatterplot | bubble | XY heatmap
  • RANKING: ordered bar | ordered column | ordered proportional symbol | dot strip plot | slope | lollipop | bump
  • DISTRIBUTION: histogram | dotplot | dot strip plot | barcode plot | boxplot | violin plot | population pyramid | cummulative curve | frequency polygrams | beesurran
  • CHANGE-OVER-TIME: line | column | column & line timeline | slope | area chart | candlestick | fan chart (progjections) | connected scatterplot | calendar heatmap | priestley tiemline | circle timeline | vertical tiemline | sersmogram | streamgraph
  • MAGNITUDE: column | bar | paired column | paired bar | marimekko | proportional symbol | isotype (pictogram) | lollipop | radar | parallel coordinates | bullet | grouped symbol
  • PART-TO-WHOLE: stacked column/bar | marimekko | pie | donut | treemap | voronia | arc | gridplot | venn | waterfall
  • SPATIAL: basic chloropleth (rtio/rate) | proportional symbol (count/magnitude) | flow map | contour map | equalized cartogram | scaled cartogram (value) | dot density | heat map
  • FLOW: sonkey | waterfall | chord | network
