2015 PANAM GAMES - TORONTO
July 2015 Panam Games in Toronto were a success. The Canadian water ski team skied very well, earning 9 medals, 4 gold and 5 silver, along with 2 fourth place finishes! The Canadian Waterski-Wakeboard Team:
  • Rusty MALINOSKI [gold wakeboard]
  • Ryan DODD [gold jump]
  • Jaret LLEWELLYN [silver overall | silver trick | 4th jump]
  • Whitney MCCLINTOCK [gold overall | gold slalom | silver trick | silver jump]
  • Jason MCCLINTOCK [silver slalom | 4th trick]
