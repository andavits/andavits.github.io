Anda is a Data Scientist, specializing in behaviour data measurement and modelling, within a DataOps framework.
Anda has been an interactive web developer since the first experiments on smart user workstations, having created marketing, accounting and tracking platforms for large corporations as Ontario New Home Warranty Program, IBM, Bank of Montreal and RIM-BlackBerry; as well as large online education programs for World Wildlife Federation, Toronto Maple Leafs, K12 Global, and her own self-help, sport psychology resource platform, called Self-Directed Coaching.
Anda rooted her career as a mainframe system administrator-programmer and as an academic overachiever in science of psychology.
Anda enjoys being in nature, cartooning, and choir.
