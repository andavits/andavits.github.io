DEFINITIONS

News walk-through
Reporter walks us through complicated documents and procedures, citing direct source, so we can look it up
News opinions
Reporter states own conclusions
Journalists often explore "other side of story" but that is not always how nature and the truth is balanced. Be cognizant of that.
News as entertainment (tabloids):
These are fun but again are fiction or partial truths dramatized. In other words, do not take this as real.
Overall: Back to first point
Basically, read the news, but wait until all sources are credible, before putting energy into it by reacting.
