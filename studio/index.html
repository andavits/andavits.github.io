Nod to the Greats
Illustrative journal
Since childhood, I have been in awe with "the greats" - the California animators of the mid1900s. Their ability to exagegrate expressions, hiding nothing, creates a charming truth and honesty in their characters. No matter if the character is "good" or "evil", there is integrity. We know exactly who they are. This sketchbook is a nod the "great ones".
