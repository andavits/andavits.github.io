Trīnīši - Triplets

Trīnīši is an illustration I made in 2000. Acrylic on stretched canvas.
Trīnīši means triplets. They are the anthropomorphic godeses of ancient Latvia. I gave them names. "Saule deja" (Sun dancing) is on top and is the essence of life. "Laima deja" (Luck dancing) and "Māra deja" (Māra dancing) is the goddess of earth. Th
Saule is the essence of life.
Laima is fate and luck.
Māra looks after all that lives on earth.
Veļa mother looks after all in the after life.
