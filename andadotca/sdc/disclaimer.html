PROMOTING CONSTRUCTIVE DIALOGUE, ETHICS
The following is common sense, but always needs to be formally stated. Why? Because anyone can give answers; what you need are the right answers. Quick answers to everything may increase one's own comfort level in the world, but for personal best perfomances, one needs non-threatening constructive dialogue.
Straight-forward talk
Communication on a straight-forward manner promotes good reasoning and reality checking.
No Self Promotion
This would be hypocritical! Specifically,
  • no persuading tactics
  • no manipulative guru A manipulative guru is someone who systematically leaves out important information, leaving you no choice but to go back to this person for every little concern.
  • no tips "If this, then do that" This conforms to one prototype. The problem is that there is no prototype for expert athletes. See overview
